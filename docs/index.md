# Kris Reynolds: MSc Portfolio
I am a member of the Technical Team within the Digital Services department at Chelmsford City Council. In this role, I contribute to the delivery and maintenance of critical digital infrastructure that underpins council services. My responsibilities span a broad range of technical domains, including cyber security, network management, application support, and database administration.

This multidisciplinary role requires a proactive and analytical approach, enabling me to diagnose and resolve complex technical issues, support business-critical systems, and ensure the reliability, performance, and security of digital services. Through this work, I have developed a strong foundation in IT operations alongside a practical understanding of how technology supports organisational objectives within the public sector.
